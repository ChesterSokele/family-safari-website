Chef K's Culinary Experience

Our restaurant is led by the talented Chef K and team, offering a unique blend of local Zambian flavors and international cuisine. From our famous "chi fish" to succulent grilled meats, every dish is prepared with passion and the freshest ingredients.

Food Policy: Guests are welcome to bring their own food for braais/bbqs. However, we do not allow outside drinks - enjoy our wide selection of beverages and cocktails instead!
